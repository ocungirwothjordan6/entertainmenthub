Premier League Gameweek 1 Review

Posted: Aug 3, 2025 | 2:15 PM

Thrilling Start to the Season

The Premier League returned with a bang in Gameweek 1 of the 2025/26 season. Goals, drama, and shock results ensured that the opening weekend lived up to expectations.

Key Highlights

Manchester United vs Arsenal: Arsenal edged past Manchester United with a 1–0 victory at Old Trafford, showing early signs of title intent.

Manchester City vs Tottenham: Spurs stunned the defending champions Manchester City with a 2–0 away win, shaking up the early title predictions.

Liverpool: Arnes Slot's men were involved in thrillers — first a 4–2 win over Bournemouth and later a dramatic 3–2 victory at Newcastle, showing their attacking firepower.

Chelsea: The Blues were dominant with a 5–1 win at West Ham but stumbled in a 0–0 draw against Crystal Palace.

Newcomers: Burnley and Nottingham Forest impressed with big wins, while Leeds United grabbed a tight victory over Everton.
